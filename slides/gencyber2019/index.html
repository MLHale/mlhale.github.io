Question 3: How can I Better connect First Principles throughout my camps?

slides.mlhale.com/gencyber2019/

REtrofit

If you already have curricular content, go through it and identify points in the instructional design where first principles can be reinforced.

If you can't find first principles in your lessons, ask yourself if the lesson is really valuable or if the instructional design/learning objectives are the right ones?

Design To

If you are starting fresh, design your lessons around reinforcing certain principles. Ask yourself if the examples in your lessons are the best ones? Do they provide conceptual clarity? Do they generalize?

 

Design reinforcement moments in active learning activities.
